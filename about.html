BACKGROUND

Dutch health has been operating since 1992 as an International Turn-Key Solution Provider in the Medical field. We supply medical equipment packages, ambulances, mobile hospitals, integral clinics and all related auxiliary services. We have our own factory producing ambulances, water-ambulances and mobile hospitals in the Netherlands. Dutch Health provides a complete range of professional services to hospitals and other health facilities.
